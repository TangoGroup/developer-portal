---
title: 'Customize AI Assistants'
description: 'Configure, customize, and integrate your content into your Assistant.'
---

Gloo AI Studio is the jumping point for managing your AI Assistants. In the Studio Home page, go to the Solutions section and select Assistant. This will take you to a site where you can configure and maintain your assistant until the integration is finished.

<Info>
    A this time you'll have to set up a separate login on the Assistants site. There are plans in the future to consolidate logins so you only need one account to for Studio and Assistants.
</Info>

This guide covers the primary management tasks you will perform in the Assistants site.
