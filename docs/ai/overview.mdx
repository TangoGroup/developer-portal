# Core Values Make Us Different to the Core

While other AI companies strive to advance AI technology for profit or for the sake of advancement, Gloo AI is different. We are centered on human flourishing, and our differentiators stem from that belief in redemptive AI.

![](/images/docs/ad56a81adbfcb4d42dfc0a0311096bbcc3f6fc2b8143816c1220b15c655c864a-Gloo_AI_Differences.jpg)

Gloo AI's mission is to make trusted, values-aligned AI accessible to the entire world. Join us in our mission by [learning more](https://docs.ai.gloo.com/update/docs/getting-started#/) and building with our tools.
